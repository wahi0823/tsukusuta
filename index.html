About Japan

Welcome to Japan.

Japan is an island nation located in East Asia, made up of approximately 6,800 islands. The four major islands — Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, and Kyushu — form the heart of the country. Surrounded by the Pacific Ocean, Japan is blessed with rich nature and diverse climates.

From the snow-covered landscapes of the north to the subtropical islands of the south, Japan enjoys four distinct seasons. The unique climate and geography of each region have deeply influenced local lifestyles and cultural traditions.

Japan is a land where ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology exist in harmony. From the temples and shrines of Kyoto and Nara to the skyscrapers and robotics of Tokyo — from the delicate craftsmanship passed down through generations to world-renowned precision engineering — Japan continues to create its future with the spirit of “Onko-Chishin” (learning from the past to innovate the new).

In our pavilion, we explore the themes of “connection,” “coexistence with nature,” and “the spirit of harmony.” We invite you to discover the values that Japan holds dear, and the ideas we hope to share as we walk into the future together.

A small island nation, filled with great wisdom and gentle kindness —
That is the message we bring to you, from Japan.

About This Site

Here, we invite you to explore some of the unique and fascinating aspects of Japanese culture through four special themes:

  • Hiroshi Mori: A bestselling Japanese novelist known for his thought-provoking mysteries and philosophical storytelling.

  • Kimono: Japan’s traditional clothing, which reflects the beauty of nature, the seasons, and centuries of craftsmanship.

  • Traditional architecture: Including shrines and temples that reveal Japan’s spiritual heritage and aesthetic harmony with nature.

  • The Emperor of Japan: A symbol of unity and continuity in Japanese history and society.

Each section of this site offers insights into these rich cultural topics, carefully explained so that everyone — regardless of background — can enjoy and understand them.

Whether you're a first-time visitor to Japanese culture or already a fan, we hope this site helps deepen your appreciation for the stories, values, and beauty that shape Japan.

Let’s take a journey together into the heart of Japan.
